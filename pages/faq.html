FAQ

Q: What will I receive when I finish my commission?

A:You will receive the highest quality digital images via email attachment in JPG and PNG

Q: How do I commission you? What does that process look like?

A:The easiest way to explain this is a list!

  1. The first step is to contact me by email which I leave the link at the end.
  2. Ideas are discussed (type of commission, scene, background etc.. ) and I will ask you for references.
  3. Two thumbnails will be created for you to choose, then modified until it is what you are looking for.
  4. Payment is made in full through PayPal.
  5. In case the commission is made I will notify you with a note the price and you have 3 days to make the first payment.
  6. A first payment of 50% of the agreed amount will be made and after the commission is finished I will notify you with Gmail and you have 2 to 3 days to make the last payment 50% (if the client wishes, he can pay 100% of the entrance fee).
  7. Each commission will get the Chibi for free.
  8. Updates will be sent every time the customer requires it.
  9. You have the possibility to correct up to 3 times
  10. The finished piece in png and jpg format will be sent attached by Gmail completion date up to 3 days minimum and maximum up to 5 days.
